❏ Preface

In recent years, with the increase in admission rates and the addition of new departments in colleges and universities, the situation of students renting outside has become more serious due to the inadequate matching of existing school buildings. As a result, the number of safety and dispute issues has also increased. Not only has it caused great inconvenience to students' lives, but also, in various types of problems, it is found that most of the situations faced by students are similar or even repeatedly occurring. It is evident that the problems caused by renting houses are completely unfamiliar and often overlooked by young students who lack social experience, and this part often seriously interferes with students' study emotions. Therefore, in order to give students a basic understanding of rental-related issues, we have specially organized these issues into a practical Q&A format, hoping to provide some help to students through explanations of some practical examples.
