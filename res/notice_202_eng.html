Notes on Returning the Rental Home

  1. After moving out, please go to check the equipment in the house (including furniture). Is it in good condition? or reduced?
  2. Are the pipes, toilets running smoothly?
    ➥Video recording for evidence.
  3. Are the leftovers and trash cleaned up?
    ➥If you have left anything behind, it will be treated as garbage unless you communicate with the landlord in advance.
  4. Do you owe a management fee? (Settlement fee will be deducted from the deposit)
    ➥ Management fee should get a receipt for retention.
  5. Keys must be returned on the day of handover.
    ➥ If the landlord avoids meetings or delays for any reason, you can send a double-registered letter with a deposit letter.
