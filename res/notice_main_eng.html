1. Consider safety issues when looking at a house, such as exterior cracks, fire, and burglary prevention, and escape routes or stairwells. If the building's exterior is cracked, escape routes or stairwells are blocked with debris, or burglar-proof windows are reserved for escape openings, these are potential safety hazards. Additionally, water heaters should not be installed inside the house.
  2. Please invite a few friends to go with you when you visit the house to protect your safety. You can also listen to the opinions of your classmates.
  3. Please be careful if the roof is a legal addition to the house.
  4. When paying the deposit, ask the landlord to provide a receipt that includes the renter's address, the landlord's name, the deposit amount, the ID number, and the scheduled start date. This receipt should also include the rental address, the expected starting date, and the deposit payment date. Having this receipt is important to avoid any disputes or issues with the landlord not acknowledging the rental agreement after receiving the deposit.
  5. Before signing the contract, ask the landlord to provide the building's title and identity card to confirm their ownership. If the landlord is a second owner, ask for a power of attorney.
  6. When signing a contract, it is essential to clearly define the responsibilities for repairing and maintaining common facilities and furniture in the room to avoid problems with the landlord in the event of equipment damage.
  7. Landlords with special requirements, such as no cooking in the house, and no pets, etc. must be listed in the contract to avoid problems.
  8. Payments for water, electricity, gas, internet, fourth station, etc. must be To avoid unnecessary problems, you must indicate on the rental contract.
  9. When signing a contract, please avoid verbal contracts. You can obtain a free school contract from our Leasing Center if you need to do so.
  10. After renting, you can ask the landlord or change the locks yourself to avoid the previous tenant If the previous tenant has a spare key, it will affect your safety and rights.
